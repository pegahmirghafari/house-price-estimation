Get a rough estimate of what your property is worth

Please keep in mind this app is not 100% accurate, this model only takes 'Overall Quality', 'Number of Bath', 'Garage Area', 'Lot Area','Year Built',and 'Number of Bedrooms', into consideration when giving an estimate, however a huge factor is the location of the property.
